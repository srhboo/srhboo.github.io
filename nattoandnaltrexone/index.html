fragments and notions of chosen home from childhood through to the pandemic and beyond, morphing

sobriety arrived like a friend I thought I had lost forever. to ease the strangeness was to rebuild a life, every day. all the rituals that had been seeped in rum needed new ones to take their place. and so, new midnight friends. to the measured barrier of naltrexone and the sensory joys of natto, I am indebted.
